Privacy Policy — DrCapsule

Effective date: 2026-03-01

This Privacy Policy explains how the DrCapsule mobile application (package: dr_capsule) handles information.

1. Contact

If you have questions about privacy, contact us at: jakatorawow@gmail.com.

2. Data we collect

DrCapsule does not collect or transmit personal data to any server. The app is designed to work offline and does not require an account.

  • Local game data: scores, settings, statistics, and game history are stored only on your device.
  • No analytics: we do not use analytics tools to track users.
  • No ads: the app does not show ads and does not share data with advertisers.

3. Device permissions

The app does not require access to sensitive permissions (such as contacts, location, or SMS). Any standard permissions requested by the system may be required by the platform to run the app.

4. Children’s privacy

The app is not intended to collect personal information from children. Since we do not collect data or operate accounts, we also do not profile users.

5. Sharing of data

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties — because we do not collect it.

6. Data retention

Local game data remains on your device until you delete it (for example, by uninstalling the app or clearing app data).

7. Security

Because the app runs offline and does not transmit data to servers, security mainly depends on your device. We recommend keeping your OS up to date and using a screen lock.

8. Changes to this policy

If we add features that require data collection in the future (such as accounts, cloud sync, analytics, or crash reporting), we will update this policy and change the effective date.

9. Your rights

If you have questions, contact us at jakatorawow@gmail.com. Since we do not process personal data on servers, requests regarding access/deletion generally apply to local data on your device.

This document is provided for an offline app without accounts and without analytics.
